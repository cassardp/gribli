# Gribli Support

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I play Gribli?

Match 3 or more identical symbols by swapping adjacent tiles. Chain combos to maximize your score before time runs out.

### How does the leaderboard work?

After a game, you can submit your score with a display name to compete on the global leaderboard. Submitting your score is entirely optional.

### Is an internet connection required?

No. You can play Gribli offline. An internet connection is only needed to view or submit scores to the leaderboard.

### Where is my data stored?

Your game progress and settings are stored locally on your device. Leaderboard scores are stored securely on our servers.

### How do I delete my data?

Delete the app to remove all local data from your device.
